selain math dan string method, mysql juga punya method untuk date time, https://dev.mysql.com/doc/refman/8.0/en/date-and-time-functions.html, contoh nya:
SELECT id,
    EXTRACT(YEAR FROM created_at) AS 'YEAR',
    EXTRACT(MONTH FROM created_at) AS 'MONTH'
FROM products;

atau
SELECT id,
    YEAR(created_at),
    MONTH(created_at)
FROM products;
